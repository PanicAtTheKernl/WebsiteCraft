Welcome to Pi Craft!

A small, LGBT-friendly Minecraft server.

Who We Are

This server started pretty simple: We just wanted somewhere that actually felt comfortable to log into. A lot of servers are either too chaotic or way too serious, and we wanted something in between. It's not just about Minecraft here, though. It's about having a place where people can relax, make something cool, or just talk if they feel like it. Everyone's got their own stuff going on outside the game, and sometimes it's nice to have a little escape where people actually get along. We're not perfect, and the server isn't either. But it's real, and we care about keeping it that way. If that sounds like something you'd want, you're welcome here. Visit our Join page to get started.

    How to Join

    Instructions

    1. Join our Discord server.
    2. Enable Direct Messages for our server in your Discord Privacy settings.
    3. Read the guidelines in #guidelines.
    4. Complete the application process via the Appy bot in #apply-for-whitelist.
    5. Wait for approval. This may take some time.

    Guidelines

    Last Modified: 2025-09-03

    Integrity

    Of the server
    • Fair Play. Exploits, hacks, or anything else that provide an unfair advantage are not allowed. Keep it fair for everyone.
    • Performance Impact. Avoid doing stuff that could overload the server or make it crash. It doesn't have endless power.

    Respect

    One another
    • Final Say. If staff tells you to do something, just do it. They're here to keep things running smooth, not ruin your fun. Incidents can be reviewed afterward.
    • No Griefing. Don't modify builds, steal, or otherwise grief players. If you don't ask permission then you likely don't have it.
    • Words Hurt. Harassment, bullying, and discrimination have no place here. If someone asks you to stop, just stop.

    Safety

    For everyone
    • Be Real. Don't knowingly misrepresent yourself.
    • Stranger Danger. Don't ask for or provide personally identifiable details.

    Legal

    For compliance
    • Discord's Terms of Service
    • Minecraft EULA
    • Disclaimer. We retain the right to add, change, or remove any and all content of our site and servers, in part or in full, at any time. Announcements made on Discord will serve as proper notice of changes and will become effective immediately.
    • Notice. Any depictions of gore, sexual content, or other severely obscene material and behaviour will result in an immediate ban. Criminal activity will be reported to law enforcement.
